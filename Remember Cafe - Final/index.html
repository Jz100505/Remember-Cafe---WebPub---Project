About us

Remember the Moment.

Remember Café is a cozy coffee spot along Friendship Hi-way in Angeles City, Pampanga. Designed for relaxing, studying, or catching up with friends, we serve freshly brewed coffee, frappes, fruit teas, and light bites like pastries and pasta. With our warm ambiance and friendly service, we hope to be your go-to place to sip, unwind, and make memories.
